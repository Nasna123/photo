About Us

I’m a professional Indian freelance photographer now living in Dubai capturing the perfect moments in time. We are surrounded by perfect moments. I’m here to work and make things possible for you, to share milestones using a fun timeline, to shoot as honestly as possible and capture the things that light my soul and make me happy. My mood reflects my choice of subjects. I believe in having a good time while doing what I love, in capturing your emotions, to ensure that moments are relived whenever you want and help you make them last for a lifetime. I love to capture special moments and aim to offer relaxed, fun and natural photo-shoots. From your wedding photoshoot to your child’s birthday party and your prestigious company event. I can make mistakes and create a mess, but only experiments lead to beautiful results as every great discovery was once considered a mistake to come out and be something so beautiful. A photograph is not just a photograph, it is your history captured for eternity and your life deserves being captured perfectly!
